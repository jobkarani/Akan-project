Akan-Naming-Algorithm

Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture.
Children are given their first names as a 'day name'.
This name corresponds to the day of the week in which they were born.

Male names:

  1. Monday: Kwadwo
  2. Tuesday: Kwabena
  3. Wednesday: Kwaku
  4. Thursday: Yaw
  5. Friday: Kofi
  6. Saturday: Kwame
  7. Sunday: Kwasi

Female names:

  1. Monday: Adwoa
  2. Tuesday: Abenaa
  3. Wednesday: Akua
  4. Thursday: Yaa
  5. Friday: Afua
  6. Saturday: Ama
  7. Sunday: Akosua
